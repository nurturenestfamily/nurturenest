Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy explains how NurtureNest (nurturenest-family.netlify.app) collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use NurtureNest:

  • Email address (when you subscribe to our newsletter via Brevo)
  • Usage data such as pages visited and time spent on the site (via analytics)
  • Device and browser information for site optimisation

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you our parenting newsletter (only if you subscribed)
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To understand which articles are most helpful to parents

3. Newsletter & Email

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email is stored securely with Brevo (our email platform). You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any email we send. We will never sell your email address to third parties.

4. Cookies

NurtureNest may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. These are small files stored on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic — this uses anonymous data only.

5. Third-Party Links

Our articles contain links to trusted third-party sources such as the American Academy of Pediatrics (aap.org), CDC (cdc.gov), NHS (nhs.uk), and others. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external websites.

6. Google AdSense

NurtureNest may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

NurtureNest is a resource for parents and caregivers, not for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Your email data is stored securely through Brevo, which complies with GDPR data protection standards.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at: sunnyzah85@gmail.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.

12. SSL Security Certificate

NurtureNest uses HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption) to protect all data transmitted between your browser and our website. When you see the padlock icon 🔒 in your browser's address bar, your connection to NurtureNest is encrypted and secure. This means any information you submit — including your email address for our newsletter — is transmitted securely. Our SSL certificate is provided automatically by Netlify, our hosting provider, and is renewed automatically at no cost.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please email us at: sunnyzah85@gmail.com
