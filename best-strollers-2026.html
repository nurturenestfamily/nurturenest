🛒 Which Stroller Type Do You Need?

👶

Travel System

Stroller + infant car seat combo. Best for newborns — click car seat in and out without waking baby.

✈️

Lightweight/Travel

Under 15 lbs, compact fold. Perfect for travel, errands, and families on the go.

🌆

Full-Size City

Best ride quality, large storage, grows with baby. Ideal for daily use in urban areas.

🏃

Jogging Stroller

Fixed front wheel, air-filled tires. For active parents who run with baby (6+ months).

👯

Double Stroller

Side-by-side or tandem for two kids. Essential for twins or kids close in age.

🛒 Stroller Buying Guide for New Parents

🚗

Check Your Car Trunk First

The most common mistake! Measure your trunk and compare to the stroller's folded dimensions before buying. Many full-size strollers won't fit in small car trunks.

🏙️

Match Your Lifestyle

City parents need narrow, maneuverable strollers. Suburban parents benefit from all-terrain wheels. Travelers need lightweight foldable options. There's no one-size-fits-all.

👶

Think About Newborn Compatibility

Not all strollers work from birth. Look for flat recline, infant insert, or car seat compatibility if you're buying before baby arrives.

💰

Travel Systems Save Money

Buying a stroller + car seat together as a travel system is almost always cheaper than buying them separately — and they're guaranteed to be compatible.
